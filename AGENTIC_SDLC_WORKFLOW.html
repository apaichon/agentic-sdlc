Recommended Agentic SDLC Workflow

A practical operating model for PM, Developer, Tester, and DevOps collaboration between humans and AI agents, with Git as the system of record.

Human Owns accountability, direction, trade-offs, and final approval.
AI Agent Drafts, analyzes, implements, tests, documents, and iterates faster.
Git Preserves branches, PRs, documents, decisions, and audit trail.

Practical rule: no AI output is complete until it becomes a reviewable artifact in Git.

Recommended Workflow Modes

Sync

High ambiguity

Use for architecture decisions, sprint planning, and incident triage.

Async

Clear task

Use for generating tests, docs, summaries, and straightforward implementation.

Parallel

Independent work

Use when Dev, Tester, and DevOps can work on separate files or branches.

Resync

Merge findings

Use after parallel analysis to resolve conflicts and decide next steps.
